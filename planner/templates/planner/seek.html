Seed Finder

Don't know your seed? Roll the gacha in your game, record what you get, and the finder replays every possible seed until it finds the one that deals exactly those cats.

How to record your rolls
  • Pick the gacha event you rolled on (e.g. Ultra Souls or Uberfest). Rare (gold) tickets or cat food — both work the same.
  • Roll several times — preferably 7 to 10 — and record the results. The more rolls, the more certain the answer. Fill them in below in the exact order you rolled them.
  • If you did an 11-roll on a guaranteed event, or a 15-roll (3+5+7) step-up: don't include the guaranteed uber — enter the 10 real rolls before it, and nothing rolled after it.
  • Short on tickets? Enter {{ min_rolls }} rolls and search. If several seeds match, roll one more cat, add it, and search again — repeat until one seed remains.
  • Avoid events that repeat the same cat in their pool (e.g. Metal Busters or some collabs): the duplicate slots are indistinguishable, so you'd have to try each copy in the list one by one.
  • You only need to find your seed once — from then on the planner tracks it as you roll.
