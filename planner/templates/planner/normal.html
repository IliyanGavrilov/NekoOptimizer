Normal Capsules

The Normal Cat Capsule runs on its own seed, separate from the rare gacha the planner tracks. Every normal-side machine (Catfruit, Catseye, Lucky Tickets…) reads the same normal seed, so one table predicts them all — including which pulls land a Dark Catseye during a Catseye Capsules event.

Find my normal seed
  • Roll the plain Normal Capsules several times — 8 to 12 works best — and record what you get, in order. They cost pocket XP, so this is the cheap way (the event machines would burn 12+ tickets for the same answer).
  • The finder searches the pool your Superfeline unlocked toggle (next to the machine picks below) says you have — the pools differ, so if the search finds nothing, check that toggle first.
  • The pulls must be consecutive: nothing rolled in between on any normal-side machine.
  • You only need this once — from then on the tracker follows your seed as you click the dice.
